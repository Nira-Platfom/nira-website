Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Nira ("we", "us", or "our") is an AI-powered beauty business platform operated by Nira Technologies, based in Dar es Salaam, Tanzania. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our mobile application, website, and WhatsApp bot service.

By using Nira, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

1 Information We Collect

For Business Owners (App Users)

  • Full name and email address
  • Business name, type, city, and phone number
  • Business products, services, and pricing information
  • Customer booking and order history
  • Subscription and billing information
  • Usage analytics and app activity

For Customers (WhatsApp Bot Users)

  • WhatsApp phone number
  • Name provided during booking or order placement
  • Delivery address provided during orders
  • Conversation history with the bot
  • Booking preferences and order history

2 How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and operate the Nira platform and services
  • Process bookings, orders, and customer inquiries
  • Send booking confirmations and order status updates
  • Generate business analytics and performance reports
  • Send marketing messages only when explicitly enabled by you
  • Improve our AI responses and overall service quality
  • Respond to customer support requests
  • Comply with legal obligations

3 Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

We may share data with trusted service providers:

  • Meta / WhatsApp — For delivering WhatsApp messages
  • Anthropic — For AI-powered responses. No personal data stored
  • Flutterwave — For payment processing when billing is activated
  • Neon Database — For secure, encrypted data storage
  • Railway / Vercel — For hosting our backend and website

4 Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored on encrypted servers
  • Each business's data is completely isolated from other businesses
  • Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text
  • API keys are stored securely server-side and never exposed to clients
  • All data transmission uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • We conduct regular security reviews of our platform

5 Data Retention

  • Business account data: retained while active + 90 days after deletion
  • WhatsApp conversation history: retained for 12 months
  • Order and booking records: retained for 3 years
  • You may request deletion of your data at any time

6 Your Rights

  • Access — Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction — Correct inaccurate data
  • Deletion — Request deletion of your personal data
  • Export — Download your business data
  • Opt-out — Unsubscribe from marketing at any time

7 WhatsApp Bot Users

  • Phone numbers are stored linked to the specific business contacted
  • Conversation history is visible only to that business
  • Data is never shared between different businesses on the platform
  • Customers can type STOP at any time to opt out

8 Children's Privacy

Nira is intended for business owners and adult customers. We do not knowingly collect personal information from persons under 18 years of age.

9 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notification.
